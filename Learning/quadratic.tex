\title{Quadratic Formula}
\author{Christopher Munoz}
\date{\today}

\maketitle

The quadratic formula is used to solve a quadratic equation $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$. The solution is given by:

\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
